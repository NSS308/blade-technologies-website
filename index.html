How We Work

We keep our process simple, transparent, and focused on delivering software that actually works for your business.

1

Understand the Problem

We start with a conversation to understand your current systems, workflows, and pain points. This helps us identify what needs to be built, improved, or replaced and what success looks like for you.

2

Define the Scope

Once we understand your needs, we outline a clear scope of work. This includes what the software will do, how it will be used, and how it will be maintained. We then provide a straightforward quote before any work begins.

3

Build Purpose-Built Software

We design and develop custom internal software tailored to how your team actually operates. Our focus is on reliability, clarity, and long-term usability rather than unnecessary complexity.

4

Review and Refine

We review the system with you, gather feedback, and make adjustments as needed. This ensures the software fits your workflow before it is fully deployed.

5

Deploy and Maintain

After deployment, we remain available for ongoing support, maintenance, and improvements. As your needs change, your software can evolve with you.

About Blade Technologies

Blade Technologies was founded to help organizations bridge the gap between rigid off-the-shelf software and the reality of how their operations actually run.

Many companies rely on spreadsheets, disconnected tools, or aging systems because existing software does not fully fit their workflows. We set out to change that by building internal software that is simple, purpose-built, and aligned with how teams really work.

Our approach is practical and focused. We prioritize clarity, reliability, and long-term maintainability over unnecessary complexity. Clients choose Blade because we listen first, build intentionally, and support what we create.
